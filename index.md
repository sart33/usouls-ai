# USouls AI

**Private AI chat application with full local data control**

USouls AI is a private AI chat app focused on user control, flexible interactions, and keeping all data local.

## Features

- Chat with AI characters using flexible interaction styles
- Generate images directly in the chat scene and in a dedicated gallery
- Multi-chat with several characters at the same time
- Create your own characters or use the built-in ones
- Store all chats, images and API keys locally on your device

Generated images are visible **only inside the app** and do not appear in your phone’s gallery.

## Pricing

No subscriptions. You only pay for API usage:

- Chat: **~0.2 – 1 cent per hour** (depending on message length and style)
- Image generation: **~0.5 cent per image**

Typical monthly cost is around **$2–3** for regular use.

## Settings & Capabilities

- Adjustable chat font size for comfortable reading
- Response creativity / temperature control
- Personality drift protection (keeps character consistent)
- Automatic summarization of long conversation branches (20–200 messages)
- Auto-delete for in-chat images (configurable from 7 to 60 days)
- Three image generation modes: Romance, Erotic, NSFW
- Gallery automatically switches to the current character style (tap avatar to open matching gallery)

## Privacy

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Requests are sent directly to DeepSeek (chat) and Navita AI (images)
- The developer has no access to your chats, characters or images
- Images never appear in the system gallery

## Main Advantages

- Complete privacy — everything stays on your device
- Extremely low cost
- Dark neon theme (black, pink, purple)
